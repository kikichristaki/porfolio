Your Workspace 2020

The workspace of the future is a project in collaboration with ISKU company. The aim is to develop a product or service the fulfill the needs of the future workspace. It is a large scale project undertaken by an interdisciplinary team and includes all the stages of designing and producing a commercial product. From user studies and brainstorming to conducting market research, designing, prototyping and production.

After extensive brainstorming and extensive research of what office workers need, the project is currently at the stage of prototyping and testing various ideas and solutions.
