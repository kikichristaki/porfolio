Estia

Estia is a project developed during a 3D City model Hackathon. It is based on the concept of smart buildings and suggest an approach of interaction between buildings and their inhabitants. The purpose of the app is to increase awareness regarding energy consumption and space utilization. It includes 3D building modelling and data visualization applied in the 3D models.

Technologies used: Unity3D, Rhino, Rest API
